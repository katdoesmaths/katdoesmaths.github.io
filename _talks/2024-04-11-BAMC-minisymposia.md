This minisymposia was organised by myself and [Fraser Waters](https://people.bath.ac.uk/frw22/), as we wanted to increase discussions about communication styles in mathematics. AS such we invited a range of speakers each with their own unique format for communicating mathematics.

* [Katie Chicot](https://research.open.ac.uk/people/kc2645)
* [Adam Townsend](https://adamtownsend.com/)

* [Robyn Goldsmith](https://www.lancaster.ac.uk/stor-i-student-sites/robyn-goldsmith/)
* [Christian (Kit) Yates](https://www.kityates.com/)
